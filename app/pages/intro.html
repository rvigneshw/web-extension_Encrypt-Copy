Problems with current clipboard implementation

Spyware and other malicious programs keep looking in your clipboard (because clipboard permission is not seen as a threat by many people. So Spywares make use of it and exploit them in various ways!!!) for the valuable contents such as

  • Passwords Which can be used to hack your social media accounts and in a very bad situation your entire mobile can be hacked.
  • Credit Card number and CVV Which can be used to hack your bank account make unauthorized purchases in your name or take out cash advances using your account.
  • Your OTP Which can be used fake online payments as well as to change your social media account's passwords.
  • Even the Email ID Which can be used to send spam emails which include malicious links and stuff you find inappropriate

What does this extension do?

This extension encrypts the text before storing it in the clipboard. The process flow is as follows

  • Get the selected text when you click Encrypt and Copy from the context menu (or) you can press Alt+C keyboard shortcut.
  • Encrypts the text using AES encryption
  • Stores the text hello in the clipboard
  • Even when you press Ctrl+V you can only see a random string (which is the encrypted data of the text you copied)
  • Now when spyware tries to access your clipboard they can only see your encrypted contents, So your credentials are safe even when spyware gets access to your system clipboard!
  • Well, how to get the originally copied credential back?
  • Place the cursor on any input field and right-click to get the context menu, In that menu look for Decrypt and Paste. (or) you can press Alt+P When you click that the original data you copied will be pasted in that input field.
